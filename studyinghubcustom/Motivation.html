Motivation

Elon Musk is one of the most influential people of the 21st century, and is the CEO/Founder of both Tesla and SpaceX. His work ethic is what I admire about this man the most however. Elon Musk has been putting in 85-100 hours of work per week since the age of 20. Not only this, he never gives up. Countless people told him that Tesla and SpaceX would be failure, and both times he has proven them wrong.
